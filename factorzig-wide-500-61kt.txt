# Integer factorization table

n     - a positive integer
fact  - the prime factors of n, indented by distance from the nearest prime
τ     - the number of divisors of n, τ(n)
σ     - the sum of divisors of n, σ(n)
φ     - the Euler totient of n, φ(n)
λ     - the Carmichael function of n, λ(n)
Dψ    - the Dedekind ψ(n) function
μ     - the Möbius function of n, which is 1 if n is a product of an even number of distinct primes, -1 if odd, and 0 if any prime is repeated
ω     - the number of distinct prime factors of n, ω(n)
Ω     - the total number of prime factors of n, counting multiplicity, Ω(n)
λ     - the Liouville function of n, λ(n) = (-1)^Ω(n)
M     - the Mertens function M(n)
Λ     - the von Mangoldt function of n, Λ(n)
rad   - the radical of n, the product of its unique prime factors
m*    - the prime residues of n modulo a set of primes
psym  - the symmetric primes at equal offsets around n
